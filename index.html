AerGlo Manners

It is imperative to first understand the main drivers of costs in your supply chain.

  • Supply chain decisions typically are taken at three levels: strategic, tactical and operational.
  • At the strategic level, decisions typically link to business strategy and involve high investments, high change-over lead times and longer horizons.
  • At the tactical level companies focus on adopting measures that focus on competitive needs, such as moving to a target cost structure for servicing certain markets.
  • At the operational level the major focus is operational efficiency. Decisions are typically made on a day-to-day basis under the framework defined at strategic and tactical levels.
